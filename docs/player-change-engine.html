MLB player change detection with recent-versus-prior z-scores and full-season league percentiles.

Player intelligence

What changed—and how good is it?

The Change Engine sifts six hitter and pitcher measures, finds each player’s most unusual recent movement, and keeps the full-season league context beside it.

Two lenses, one readChange z-score compares each player’s recent shift with the MLB cohort. Season percentile compares the player’s full-season level with league peers.
